Reports explicit scope calls in script files, which can be replaced with safe form (may change semantics).

Check on grammar level and semantic level.

Applicable for game types that support safe assign operators (?= in CK3/VIC3/EU5, ? = in Stellaris).

Example:

# before
owner = v

# [CK3/VIC3/EU5] after
owner ?= v

# [Stellaris] after
owner?= v

# before
exists = owner
owner = v

# [CK3/VIC3/EU5] after
owner ?= v

# [Stellaris] after
owner?= v
