## Config Repositories

Each game has two config repositories, **Built-in** (used by the plugin, may contain modifications and extensions) and **Vanilla** (the original CWTools repository, or the upstream source).

Apart from Stellaris (whose configs have diverged significantly with many additions), configs for other games are largely kept in sync between the plugin and CWTools.
